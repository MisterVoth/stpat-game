3) Build the Legend

Sequencing + creative writing.

← Home / QR Grid

How to play

  • Choose a level. Complete the sequencing/writing task.
  • Keep it short and clear.
  • Show your final story to the teacher.
Goal: Put the sentences in order. Then add one new sentence at the end.
  1. The leprechaun has a pot of gold.
  2. It begins to rain.
  3. A rainbow appears in the sky.
  4. A boy sees the rainbow.
  5. The boy follows the rainbow.
  6. He finds the leprechaun.

Teacher check: correct order + 1 new sentence = 1 point.

Goal: Put the story in order. Then add two new sentences about what happens next. Use two words from the list.
  1. The villagers are worried about bad luck.
  2. A strange man arrives in the village.
  3. He carries a small green bag.
  4. The crops have not grown this year.
  5. He tells them a story about hope.
  6. The villagers listen carefully.

Use 2 words from this list: celebrate • surprise • promise • village • tradition

Teacher check: correct order + 2 added sentences + required words = 2 points.

Goal: Create your own mini legend (5–7 sentences). Include:
  • 2 characters at minimum
  • a problem,
  • a solution,
  • a rainbow, a choice, and
  • one modal verb (must/should/might/could).

Use 3 words from the list below.
Use 3 words: fortune • journey • secret • brave • mistake • promise • discover

Teacher check: meets requirements + clear story = 3 points.

Teacher scoring: Level A = 1 point, Level B = 2 points, Level C = 3 points. Optional: +1 collaboration token (cap per team or class).
