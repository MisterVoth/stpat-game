How to play

  • Choose a level. Work quickly and carefully.
  • Level A/B: choose the correct form. Level C: transform sentences.
  • Write your answers, your name, and your ID on a paper and hand it in at International Office S100.
Goal: Choose the correct verb form (6 items). Each correct = 1 “gold coin”.
  1. People (wear / wears) green.
  2. The parade (start / starts) at 10.
  3. Many people (is / are) happy.
  4. He (like / likes) the music.
  5. They (eat / eats) potatoes.
  6. The river (turn / turns) green.

Teacher check: 5–6 correct = 1 point.

Goal: Choose the best tense/form (6 items). Then explain ONE choice in a short phrase.
  1. St. Patrick (was / is) born in the 5th century.
  2. Many people (celebrate / celebrated) yesterday.
  3. Cities (organize / organized) parades every year.
  4. Last year, we (went / go) to the festival.
  5. People (have celebrated / celebrate) this holiday for centuries.
  6. Today, many families (join / joined) the parade.

Teacher check: 5–6 correct + a clear explanation of one item = 2 points.

Goal: Transform each sentence without changing meaning (4 items). Keep grammar clear.
  1. People celebrate the holiday in many countries.
    → The holiday __________ in many countries.
  2. Many people wear green.
    → Green __________ by many people.
  3. Because it is traditional, people wear green.
    → People wear green __________ it is traditional.
  4. The parade is exciting.
    → The parade is very __________.

Teacher check: 3–4 strong transformations = 3 points.

Teacher scoring: Level A = 1 point, Level B = 2 points, Level C = 3 points. Optional: +1 collaboration token (cap per team or class).
