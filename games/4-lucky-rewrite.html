4) Lucky Rewrite

Accuracy → vocabulary upgrade → fluency.

How to play

  • Choose a level and rewrite the paragraph.
  • Focus on clarity. You don’t need perfection.
  • Show your “before → after” to the teacher.
Goal: Fix 2 spelling erros, 6 capital letters, and 4 grammar errors.
st patrick's day is on march 17. people wears green cloths and go to parade. many people is very happy and eat potatos and drinks green beers.

Teacher check: clear corrections to main errors = 1 point.

Goal: Improve grammar AND replace at least 3 simple words (e.g., good, very, big, fun, people) with better vocabulary .
St. Patrick’s Day is a good holiday. Many people go to big parades. It is very fun and people feel very good. There are big crowds and good music.

Teacher check: grammar improved and 3+ word upgrades = 2 points.

Goal: Rewrite to sound more natural, adding words and phrases as necessary. Combine at least two sentences and use one connector (because/although/however/while/in addition/where/when).
St. Patrick’s Day started in Ireland. Now it is celebrated in many countries. People wear green. Some cities dye rivers green. There are parades. Many people enjoy the holiday.

Teacher check: combined sentences + transition word + improved flow = 3 points.

Teacher scoring: Level A = 1 point, Level B = 2 points, Level C = 3 points. Optional: +1 collaboration token (cap per team or class).
