How to play

  • Choose a level. Unscramble the words.
  • Level B and C include a short writing task.
  • Show your answers to the teacher for points.
Goal: Unscramble all 5 words.

Teacher check: 5 correct = 1 point.

Goal: Unscramble all 5 words, then write one sentence using two of them.

Teacher check: correct words + sentence = 2 points.

Goal: Unscramble all 5 words, then write 2–3 sentences answering: Why do people celebrate St. Patrick’s Day? Use at least two words.

Teacher check: correct words + clear explanation = 3 points.

Teacher scoring: Level A = 1 point, Level B = 2 points, Level C = 3 points. Optional: +1 collaboration token (cap per team or class).
